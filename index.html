Wrapping 101

Straight answers
from the owner.

New to wraps? Our founder breaks down the questions we hear every day — cost, care, longevity and what actually makes a good install.

How much does it cost to wrap a car?

A full colour-change wrap starts at $2,599 and scales with your vehicle's size, the material and coverage. Smaller jobs like a hood wrap ($280) cost far less. You get an exact quote before we start.

How long does a car wrap last?

With proper care, a quality wrap lasts 5–7 years. We use premium cast films built to handle Canadian winters and summer sun.

Will a wrap damage my paint?

No — done right, it protects your factory paint and peels off cleanly. That's why wraps are so popular on leased and resale cars.

Wrap vs. paint protection film (PPF)?

A wrap changes the look and colour. PPF is a clear, self-healing layer that shields against rock chips. Many clients do both.

How long does an install take?

A full wrap is usually 3–5 days for a proper panel-off install. Tint and smaller accents are often same-day.

Can I wash it after?

Wait 10 days for the adhesive to cure, then hand wash. We include a complimentary check-up after your wrap.
